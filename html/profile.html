Dr Patrick Simon Lao (MBSS)



Fully licensed and certified, Dr. Patrick Simon Lao has been performing plastic surgery in Singapore since 2005.

Dr. Patrick received his plastic surgical training in Singapore before completing a fellowship at Taiwan's renowned Chang Gung Memorial Hospital, where he focused on craniofacial cosmetic and reconstructive plastic surgery. He completed more aesthetic plastic surgery training in the USA and Korea.

Qualifications
  • Fellow of the Academy of Medicine, Singapore (Plastic Surgery)
  • Master of Medicine (Surgery) NUS Member of Royal College of Surgeons Edinburgh
  • MBBS Singapore NUS
  • Master of Bioengineering
Committees
  • Chair, Chapter of Plastic, Reconstructive and Aesthetic Surgeons, College of Surgeons, Singapore
  • Committee Member, Singapore Society of Cosmetic Aesthetic Surgeons
  • Committee Member, Cleft Lip and Palate Association of Singapore
  • Member, Singapore Association of Plastic Surgeons
Area of Speclisation

Due to his expertise and experience, Dr Patrick has been earmarked by Quanta (Discovery Pico & Ruby) and Galderma (Dysport & Restylane Fillers) as a trainer for up-and-coming aesthetic doctors.

Nose augmentation and anti-aging treatments including the Profhilo, Ultraformer HIFU, and Thermage are Dr. Patrick's areas of greatest expertise. He tailors his treatments to each patient's ageing and facial issues. He strongly believes that aesthetics may considerably prolong youth and make the aging process more pleasant and graceful.

Dr Bob Tang Li Wei (MBSS)



Dr. Bob earned a Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery degree from the National University of Singapore in 2007.

After receiving his Graduate Diploma in Aesthetic Medicine, he became a member of the American Academy of Aesthetic Medicine.

Based on his clinical experience of more than a decade, he believes that the health of the skin is the foundation of beauty. His strategy combines the use of energy-based devices and specialized skincare products. For his patients, he uses techniques like laser and High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) to lay the groundwork for healthy skin.

He has also performed extensive research on body fat reduction treatments and face lifting surgery over the years with Aestica.

Qualifications
  • Fellow of the Academy of Medicine, Singapore (Plastic Surgery)
  • Master of Medicine (Surgery) NUS Member of Royal College of Surgeons Edinburgh
  • MBBS Singapore NUS
  • Master of Bioengineering
Committees

  • Member, Singapore Association of Plastic Surgeons
Area of Speclisation

His top priorities have always been continued learning and research. He served on the organizing committee for the largest aesthetic medicine conference in Southeast Asia, the Singapore Society of Aesthetic Medicine Conference. He has been chosen to serve on the Singapore Society of Aesthetic Medicine's executive committee in appreciation of his contributions to the local aesthetic medicine community.
